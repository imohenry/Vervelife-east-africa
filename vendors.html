How to Become a Vendor
  1. The vendor must have a Quickteller Business Account.
  2. If the Vendor does not have a Quickteller Business Account, you can register here
  3. The vendor must have an active social media page with an active community.
  4. The vendor’s business must be registered by CAC.
  5. The vendor must be willing to give a 5% discount to Verve Card users on-site.
  6. Vendor must use Quickteller Products only (POS, e-Cash wallet).
  7. The vendor should be ready to drive traffic to the brand page.
  8. The vendor should be ready and available for a product inspection/ product verification.
  9. Only products on our vendor categories can be showcased (Click here to access the categories)
  10. The vendor can only choose one out of the two venues to showcase products(Agege or Landmark Event Centre)
