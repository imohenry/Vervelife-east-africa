About Verve Life 5.0

TheVerveLife

The VerveLife Platform is synonymous with lifestyle, wellness, and the biggest fitness carnival to happen in Nigeria.
The first edition started in 2017, the platform has since expanded to a widely anticipated annual event and expressions across various wellness touch points including the VerveLife Dance Challenge and VerveLife Fitness Week.
The key objective is to become our customers’ go-to lifestyle platform that encompasses nutrition, exercise, and wellness leveraging on the identified touch points of the Verve Customer, which are fitness, fun, and healthy eating.
After conquering Nigeria as the biggest fitness party with 4 editions, we are not stopping, we are forging ahead to own the space as Africa’s Biggest Fitness Party.
VerveLife 5.0 is focused on reiterating the need to go hard at staying fit, repeat, and never stop the fitness journey. “Never Stop” is the theme for 2022 and all the activities lined up for the event are focused on reiterating our commitment to a wellness lifestyle.
